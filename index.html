Our Gym Trainers

Our experienced gym trainers offer personalized support to help you reach your fitness goals. With tailored workout plans and expert guidance, they'll motivate you every step of the way. Get ready to transform your body with their dedicated assistance.

GATETE Jean

Trainer

Jean, a certified personal trainer, specializes in strength and conditioning programs tailored to help clients achieve their fitness goals, whether it's building muscle, losing weight, or improving overall health and wellness.

MUKARUGWIZA Sandrine

Trainer

Sandrine is a passionate fitness instructor with expertise in high-intensity interval training (HIIT) and group fitness classes. She motivates and inspires clients to push past their limits and achieve remarkable results.

NKUSI David

Trainer

David, a dedicated fitness coach, specializes in functional training and corrective exercise techniques. He focuses on improving mobility, flexibility, and overall functional movement patterns to enhance clients' physical performance and prevent injuries.

MUGABO Solange

Trainer

Solange is a certified yoga instructor and holistic wellness coach. She combines traditional yoga practices with modern fitness principles to help clients achieve physical strength, mental clarity, and emotional balance.
